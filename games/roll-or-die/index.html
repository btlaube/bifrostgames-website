Roll or Die

How to Play:

  1. Roll Dice
  2. Spend the values you roll to:
    1. Heal
    2. Damage your opponent
    3. Or buy more Rolls
  3. Don't run out of Health or Rolls or it's game over!

Controls

Space: to roll dice

Mouse click and drag: dice faces to the action slots on the right

Made as a solo project in 48 hours for the GMTK Game Jam 2022 with the theme “Roll of the dice”.
