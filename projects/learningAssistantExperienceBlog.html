Answering A Request from A Learning Assistant

Tags: "Issues Resolved", "Lessons and Skills"

  • The Learning Assistant asked to see a solution to the coding assignment to better help students.
  • I got solution code and gave it to the other LAs and made sure to explain the best way to use the code to assist in your teaching
    • Don't show the code!
    • This solution is not the only solution
    • Don't make them do it this way if they have a different way that works
    • Stay vague and try to lead the student to the answer as opposed to telling them outright.

Learning How Students Prefer to Learn

Tags: "Lessons Learned"

  • Students created a different system for office hours
  • In the past students would come and go during my office hours
  • This semester I would have a group of students present for the entirety of my office hours.
  • Students used the white board system that I had only seen larger classes use
  • This class is larger this semester
  • Made office hours go smoothly and I helped one student after the other
