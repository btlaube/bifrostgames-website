About Me

Hi there!

My name is Ben Laube (or BifrostGames) and I am currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. My passion is video game development and ever since I was young, I've been captivated by the endless possibilities of video games, and it was that passion that led me to pursue a degree in computer science.

Throughout my studies, I've gained a solid foundation in programming languages such as C#, Python, and C++. I've also taken courses in data structures, algorithms, and software engineering, which have allowed me to create my own video games. I have been making games for years, from block-based visual programming like Scratch to high-level game engines like Unity and Godot.

Outside of the classroom, I spend a lot of my free time playing and analyzing video games, which has taught me what makes games engaging and enjoyable. I'm constantly seeking out new ways to push the boundaries of game design and development, and I'm always excited to learn new skills and techniques that will help me achieve that goal.

I hope to work in the video game industry as a developer or designer, creating innovative and exciting games that capture the imaginations of players around the world. Whether it's developing a new game or refining an existing one, I'm committed to bringing my passion and expertise to every project I work on.
